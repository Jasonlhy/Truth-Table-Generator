Truth Table Generator

This is a truth table generator helps you to generate a Truth Table. All you need is to input a formula, such as: a and b, a or b, a or not b, etc. Right now, it only supports the operator of and, or , not. However, it also supports the buckets, which means you can enter the formula like: (a or not b) and a. For, more information, please check out the syntax section

Syntax

You can enter the formula with variables using any names without spaces. such as jason or sam. You can also enter buckets to specify the highest precedence, (jason or tommy) or (jason and not tommy). The evaluation order of operator in the formula is according to the precedence table.
