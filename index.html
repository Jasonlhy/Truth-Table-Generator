Truth Table Generator

This is a truth table generator helps you to generate a Truth Table. All you need is to input a formula, such as: a and b, a or b, a or not b, etc. Right now, it only supports the operators: and, or , not, =>. However, it also supports the brackets, you can enter the formula like: (a or not b) and a. For, more information, please check out the syntax section

Input the formula, for example: a and b




Truth Table Generated



Syntax

You can enter the formula with variables named with any words without spaces except the reserve words , For example, you can name the formula using the names of your friends, jason or sam. You can also enter brackets to specify the highest precedence, (jason or tommy) or (jason and not tommy). The evaluation order of operators in the formula is according to the operator table.

Operator Table

The lower number of precedence in the table means the higher precedence for evaluation. Therefore, not is evaluated before and, and is evaluated before or, etc.

Operator Meaning Example Precedence
not AND operation not a 1
and OR operation a and b 2
or INCLUSIVE OR operation a or b 3
=> IMPLIES operation a => b 4


Reserved words

These are the words that you cannot use to name a variable inside the formula.

  • not
  • and
  • or
  • =>
  • (
  • )
